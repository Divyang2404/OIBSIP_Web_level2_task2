About Sushant Singh Rajput

Sushant Singh Rajput (1986–2020) was an Indian actor, engineer, and lifelong learner whose journey continues to inspire millions. Before entering the entertainment industry, he was an outstanding student with a deep interest in mathematics, physics, and science.

He secured an impressive All India Rank 7 in the Delhi College of Engineering (DCE) entrance examination and pursued Mechanical Engineering at Delhi College of Engineering (now Delhi Technological University). Although he later left engineering to follow his passion for acting, his academic excellence reflected his intelligence, discipline, and determination.

From television to Bollywood, Sushant proved that success comes from hard work and the courage to follow one's dreams. His performances in films like Kai Po Che!, M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story, Chhichhore, and Dil Bechara earned him admiration from audiences worldwide.

Beyond acting, he was passionate about astronomy, artificial intelligence, coding, books, and space exploration. His curiosity and love for learning continue to inspire students, dreamers, and young professionals around the world.

Education & Academic Excellence

🎓 Engineering Background

Sushant Singh Rajput completed his schooling in Patna and later moved to Delhi. He excelled in academics and developed a strong interest in science and mathematics.

He secured All India Rank 7 in the entrance examination for Delhi College of Engineering (DCE) and enrolled in Mechanical Engineering. Although he eventually chose acting as his career, his academic achievements continue to inspire students across the country.
